Biography of Wernher Von Braun

An aerospace engineer and space architect

Timeline of Wernher Von Braun's life:

  • MAR 23, 1912 - Wernher was born.
  • JAN 25, 1925 - Wernher attended boarding school at Ettersburg castle.
  • FEB 1, 1944 - Wernher designed and started the V-2 rocket program for the Nazis.
  • FEB 24, 1945 - He surrendered to the Americans and made rockets for the USA.
  • MAY 14, 1952 - Braun published ideas for a space station.
  • FEB 1, 1958 - Braun's explorer 1 got put orbit.
  • JUL 29, 1958 - Braun's Saturn program went under development.
  • JUL 29, 1958 - NASA was established.
  • FEB 20, 1962 - Braun's redstone missile launched the frist american.
  • JAN 27, 1967 - There was a fire on the apollo 1 killing all of the crew.
  • JUL 16, 1969 - Braun's Saturn V lifts the apollo 11 up into the air.
  • JUL 20, 1969 - Apollo 11 lands on the moon.
  • JUN 16, 1977 - Braun dies of pancreatic cancer.
